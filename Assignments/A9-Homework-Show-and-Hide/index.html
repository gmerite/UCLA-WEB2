Mickey Mouse Before/After

Mickey was created by Walt Disney and Ub Iwerks at the Walt Disney Studios in 1928.
Mickey first appeared in the short Plane Crazy, debuting publicly in the short film Steamboat Willie (1928), one of the first sound cartoons. He went on to appear in over 130 films.In 1978, Mickey became the first cartoon character to have a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame.
